About Me

Software engineering meets agricultural innovation

I'm a final-year Software Engineering student at CTU Stellenbosch with a passion for building innovative digital solutions that solve real-world problems. I specialize in turning complex challenges into simple, user-friendly applications and am committed to continuous learning in the ever-evolving field of technology.

Technical Foundation

My academic background has provided me with practical experience in Python, JavaScript, Java, HTML, and CSS, along with a solid understanding of software development principles and version control using Git and GitHub. I have hands-on experience in troubleshooting, installation, and maintenance of hardware and software systems, and am currently expanding my expertise in Java (Quarkus), PostgreSQL, and Microsoft Azure.

Agricultural Background

In addition to my IT expertise, I have extensive experience working on a farm, where I developed a strong work ethic, adaptability, and the ability to manage resources efficiently. This experience taught me the importance of teamwork, problem-solving, and maintaining impeccable records, as well as the value of technology in real-world, dynamic environments.

This background is where I developed a deep appreciation for agriculture and a solid understanding of the agricultural sector in South Africa. I am passionate about advancing technology in this sector and aim to positively impact performance and resource management through innovative solutions.

Professional Approach

I am fluent in both Afrikaans and English, and pride myself on my communication skills, professionalism, and ability to work independently or as part of a team. My goal is to contribute to impactful projects in software engineering, continue expanding my knowledge in algorithm development and system design, and make a positive difference through technology.

Currently, I'm focused on building GrazingTrack, an open-source Progressive Web App that demonstrates my ability to combine technical skills with domain knowledge to create practical, high-value solutions.
